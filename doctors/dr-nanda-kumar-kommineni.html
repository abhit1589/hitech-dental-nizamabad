Gold Medallist · First M.D.S in Nizamabad District

Dr. Nanda Kumar Kommineni is a distinguished M.D.S specialist in Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics and the founding lead of Hi-tech Dental Hospital in Nizamabad. As the first M.D.S graduate from Nizamabad district and a gold medallist in his field, he has set a benchmark for specialist dental care across the region.

With deep expertise in saving natural teeth, Dr. Nanda Kumar is widely recognized for advanced root canal treatment using modern rotary endodontic techniques. His approach focuses on precision, pain-free procedures, and long-term preservation of tooth structure — helping patients avoid extractions whenever possible.

His clinical interests include cosmetic restorations, tooth-colored fillings, crowns, and comprehensive rehabilitation of damaged or decayed teeth. He combines aesthetic dentistry with evidence-based endodontic protocols to restore both function and appearance.

As a mentor and pioneer in Nizamabad's dental community, Dr. Nanda Kumar has built Hi-tech Dental into a multi-specialty hospital where patients receive coordinated care from M.D.S experts across endodontics, oral surgery, and pediatric dentistry.

Patients value his calm demeanor, thorough explanations, and commitment to ethical, high-quality treatment. Whether managing a simple cavity or a complex endodontic case, Dr. Nanda Kumar delivers care rooted in expertise, compassion, and respect for every patient.
