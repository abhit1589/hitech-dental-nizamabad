Dr. Krishna Teja, MDS

Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon | Implantologist

Areas of Expertise

Dental Implants Full-Mouth Rehabilitation Wisdom Tooth Removal Bone Grafting Sinus Lift Impacted Tooth Surgery Oral Pathology Facial Infections Advanced Oral Surgery

Dr. Krishna Teja is one of Nizamabad's leading Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeons and Implantologists, known for his expertise in advanced dental implantology, oral surgery, and comprehensive rehabilitation of complex dental conditions.

With specialized training in implant dentistry from Novodent, Switzerland, Dr. Krishna Teja brings internationally recognized techniques and advanced treatment protocols to patients seeking world-class dental care. His commitment to continuous learning and adoption of modern surgical technologies enables him to deliver predictable, long-lasting, and aesthetically pleasing treatment outcomes.

His areas of expertise include dental implants, full-mouth rehabilitation, wisdom tooth removal, bone grafting, sinus lift procedures, impacted tooth surgeries, oral pathology, facial infections, and advanced oral surgical procedures. He is particularly sought after for managing complex implant cases and restoring smiles for patients who have lost one or more teeth.

Known for his precision, meticulous treatment planning, and patient-first approach, Dr. Krishna Teja believes that successful treatment begins with understanding each patient's unique needs and expectations. He takes time to educate patients about their treatment options and develops customized solutions that prioritize comfort, function, and long-term oral health.

At HiTech Dental, Dr. Krishna Teja combines advanced surgical expertise with compassionate care to help patients regain confidence in their smiles. His goal is not only to restore missing teeth and oral function but also to improve overall quality of life through exceptional dental and surgical care.

With a reputation for clinical excellence and a dedication to achieving the highest standards of patient care, Dr. Krishna Teja continues to be a trusted choice for dental implants, oral surgery, and advanced maxillofacial treatments in Nizamabad and the surrounding region.
