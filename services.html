Our Dental Services

Advanced treatments for every dental need, delivered with care and precision.

🦷

Teeth Cleaning & Scaling

Ultrasonic scaling and polishing to remove plaque, tartar and surface stains. Recommended every 6 months for optimal oral health.

🔧

Root Canal Treatment (RCT)

Save infected or damaged teeth with painless root canal therapy using rotary endodontics and digital imaging.

⚙️

Dental Implants

Titanium implants for permanent tooth replacement. Restore chewing ability and facial aesthetics with natural-looking results.

📐

Braces & Orthodontics

Metal braces, ceramic braces, and clear aligners to correct crooked teeth, gaps, and bite problems for all ages.

✨

Teeth Whitening

In-office laser whitening and home bleaching kits for a brighter smile. Safe for enamel with lasting results.

👑

Crowns & Bridges

Porcelain, zirconia and metal-ceramic crowns and bridges to restore damaged teeth and replace missing ones.

👶

Pediatric Dentistry

Child-friendly dental care including fluoride treatment, sealants, and habit counseling for healthy growing smiles.

💉

Tooth Extraction & Surgery

Simple and surgical extractions including wisdom teeth removal with minimal discomfort and fast recovery.

🌿

Gum Treatment

Treatment for gingivitis and periodontitis including deep cleaning, gum surgery, and maintenance therapy.
