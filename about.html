About Hitech Dental Clinic

Trusted dental care for Nizamabad and the surrounding district since 2010.

Our Story

Hi-tech Dental Hospital in Khaleelwadi is home to a team of M.D.S specialists serving Nizamabad district — from root canals and implants to gentle care for children.

Led by Dr. Nanda Kumar Kommineni, the first M.D.S graduate from Nizamabad district and a gold medallist in Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics, our clinic combines advanced specialist training with modern technology and compassionate care.

Our Mission & Values

Excellence in Care: We pursue the highest standards in every treatment, staying updated with the latest dental techniques and research.

Patient Comfort: Dental visits shouldn't be stressful. We create a calm, welcoming environment and explain every step of your treatment.

Accessibility: Quality dental care should be within reach for every family in Nizamabad district. We offer fair pricing and flexible appointment times.

Community Health: We actively participate in dental awareness camps and school programs to promote oral health across the region.
